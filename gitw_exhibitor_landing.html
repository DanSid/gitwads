Your Closing Argument

The Incentives That Make Your Product Irresistible.

Ghana's 24-Hour Economy programme doesn't just attract buyers — it gives them a financial framework that makes saying yes to your product dramatically easier.

When Your Buyer Knows The Government Is Subsidising Their Purchase, Your Booth Becomes A Closing Table.

Every conversation on the exhibition floor happens inside this context: Ghana is actively incentivising the exact investments your products serve. That transforms your pitch from "here's what we sell" to "here's what you save."

  • 50% Corporate Income Tax rebate for companies operating 24-hour shifts — your buyer's operating costs drop by half on the tax line
  • 100% Import duty and VAT waiver on machinery and equipment — your product lands at a fraction of the usual cost
  • 30% Infrastructure tax credit for private investment in roads, solar, and terminals — your buyer's project gets a 30% write-off
  • 15% Tax rebate for clean energy and bioenergy investment — solar and renewable equipment becomes even more attractive

You're not just selling a product. You're selling a product inside a government-backed incentive framework that makes the math irresistible for every buyer on the floor.
