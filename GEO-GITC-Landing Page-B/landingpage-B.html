24-Hour Economy Incentives

The Numbers That Change Every Deal.

Ghana's 24-Hour Economy programme has created one of the most competitive incentive frameworks in West Africa. These are active policies with funding and implementation timelines.

50%
Tax Rebate
Corporate Income Tax rebate for companies operating 3 shifts (24-hour operations)
100%
Duty Waiver
Full import duty and VAT waiver on machinery and equipment
30%
Tax Credit
Infrastructure tax credit for private investment in roads, solar, and terminals
12%
Interest Rate
Concessional loans with 3–10 year tenor for value chain businesses
Here's what matters: Incentives don't go to the best-qualified applicant. They go to the best-connected one — the person who understood the details because the minister explained them directly. GITW is where that happens.
